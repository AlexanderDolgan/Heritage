Investment

  • Heritage Properties Fund's business offers professional financial co-investment to support smart, aligned owner-managed property companies targeting small and medium sized transactions in northern and western Europe.
  • Heritage Properties Fund primarily seeks local operating partners that are capable of implementing change at the asset level by applying traditional property skills, such as refurbishment, development, planning improvement, change of use and active tenant management strategies.
  • Heritage Properties Fund has extensive experience across all property sectors in a variety of value creation strategies. These include individual asset repositioning, ground up development; gap funding; distressed asset acquisitions; planning plays; platform trades; and recovery capital for stressed capital structures.

«The overall investment objective is to generate an exposure to small to medium sized European real estate assets with active asset management potential, in order to deliver outsized, risk adjusted rates of return and to achieve long term capital growth without any significant risk concentrations.»
